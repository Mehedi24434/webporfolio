Special Iftary

Pizza, Chicken roll, Pasta, Dates, Chana-boot, Beguni, Alurchop

We used to eat Chana-boot, Beguni, Alurchop at our iftari during the holy Ramadan month. This is our Bengali Trade mark iftari food. Beguni is made from beshon(powder mixture of various kinds of pulse) and brinjal. Alurchop is made from potato smashed, masala, meats, egg etc. Chaana-boot is a kinds of Pulse which is boiled first then fried with masala. My mom that day made also special pizza and pasta with cheese and also her special chicken roll(using with her special trademark "samuchar patti"). These item made the whole iftari more special.

  • Occasion: Iftari
  • During: Ramadan 2020
  • Category: Homemade food

Fuchka Item

Fuchka, dal-masala, Tok

Fuchka is one of our traditional street food. During the lockdown for corona pendemic, we could not eat this food, so we missed them. For this reason, Oneday evening my mother made this item at home. Fuchka is a crispy thin layer of mixed pulse powder and rice powder with hollow ball shaped. It is used to eat with dal-masala. Which is also a kind of pulse with various kinds of masala mixture. Tok is also used for sour and sweet flavor. It is made from mainly Tamarind juice and other masalas.

  • Occassion: Sudden plan at a evening
  • During: Corona pendemic 2020
  • Category: Homemade Food
